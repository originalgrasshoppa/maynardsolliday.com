A summary of the history of the internet and all cyberspace.

        The internet was created around 1969.  It is not a physical entity. The internet is made of many computers that have combined together forming networks, which in turn are connected to other networks creating the Internet.

 

        The internet was an experiment by Advanced Research Project Agency ("ARPA"). The internet was originally called ARPANET. The computers and computer networks owned by the military, defense contractors, and university laboratories conducting defense-related research and were linked for the purpose of furthering their research rates and sharing findings and documents.

        The experiment was successful and quickly, without being urged, universities, corporations, and individuals around the world linked to the networks. The ARPANET became known as the DARPA Internet and finally the Internet.

    But the internet has possibilities has possibilities beyond mutual research.
