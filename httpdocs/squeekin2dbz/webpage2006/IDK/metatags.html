Meta tags are what you use in your html to allow search engines to find your page. They are inserted in the head of your document, and are actually invisible to the people who use your page.

Ex:

(First Bracket//)head>

(First Bracket//)meta name="description" content="Welcome to the psychotic page of Regini (also known as Fugo the Dragon or Silverus)">

(First Bracket//)meta name="keywords" content="dragon, fantasy, silverus, regini, ethan, fugo, reginidracosilver, webpage">

(First Bracket//)title>Meta Tag's

(First Bracket//)meta name="copyright" content="">

(First Bracket//)meta http-equiv="refresh" content="0; url=index.htm">

(First Bracket//)/head>
