With all the thoughts of boredom, you sit on the plane hoping for something interesting to happen.

As the plan flies along, a siren begins to sound only one hour into the flight.

Startled, you fall off your chair. Getting up you run towards the cockpit.

 

As you approach, the plane erupts in violent tremors,

and the sound of an explosion reaches you from the other side of the door to the cockpit.

You are blown back by the force, and hit the crate.  You try to get up but feel the plain falling,

 

 

so decide it would be better to hang on to the crate for safety.

 

 

"Dear Jesus," you think, then an enormous crunch with immense force pulling you down.

 You smack against the cockpit door and every thing goes dark.
