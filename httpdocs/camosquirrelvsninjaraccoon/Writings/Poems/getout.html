Get Out

Get out of my mind
Love and obsession are what I feel for you,
Though you cannot or will not love in turn.
Despite what my head tells me is right,
I harbor feelings and a place close to my heart,
Despite the obviousness that I am not what you seek.
I am a fool and afraid to share my feelings with you.
I cry inside when I think of you,
Every time I hear a song or word,
That makes me remember you.
I cry in sorrow as I know we are not meant to be.
I cry in the knowledge that I can not see you.
I cry knowing you do not cryin turn.
Life is colorful when in your presence,
But dull without your company.
REMOVE YOURSELF FROM MY MIND!
