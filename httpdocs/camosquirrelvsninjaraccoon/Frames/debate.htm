Unless all the biographical information available about him is wrong,

Fred McFeely Rogers never served in the military in any capacity, let alone as a Marine sniper

(or Navy Seal, as another version of this apocryphal story claims). Some cynical folks may be loathe to believe

that the gentle, soft-spoken host of "Mr. Rogers' Neighborhood" was exactly what he seemed, but he was.
